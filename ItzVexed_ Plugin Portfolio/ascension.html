Ascension SMP Plugin


Ascension is a custom SMP plugin where players receive a random race on join and progress through PvP-based levels with unique abilities.

  • 6 Races:
  • - Angel
  • - Demon
  • - Cyborg
  • - Beast
  • - Titan
  • - Human

  • Level system:
  • min level = 0
  • max level = 3
  • +1 kill = +1 level(+1 ability)
  • +1 death = -1 level(-1 ability)

  • Passives:
  • - Angel: No fall damage
  • - Demon: Fire Resistance
  • - Cyborg: 10% ability cooldown reduction
  • - Beast: Speed I
  • - Titan: +2 max hearts
  • - Human: 5% bonus to all stats + 5% cooldown reduction

  • Weak Abilities:
  • - Angel: dash ability
  • -Demon: burn all cobwebs in 5x5 area
  • - Cyborg: remove all negative potion effects
  • - Beast: glowing trail to track nearest player
  • - Titan: resistance for 5 seconds
  • - Human: instant fill hunger and saturation

  • Medium Abilities:
  • - Angel: Divine Judgement- Next hit triggers lightning + slight damage increase
  • - Demon: Hellfire Orb- Fireball dealing ~4 hearts through Prot 3–4
  • - Cyborg:Kinetic blast- release of absorbed damage
  • - Beast:predator's mark- applies glowing to target and glowing target take 1.35x more damage
  • - Titan: Seismic Stomp- 3×3 AoE stun
  • - Human: Adrenaline Rush- Strength II and Speed II for 10 seconds

  • Strong Abilities:
  • - Angel: instant heal to 15 hearts
  • - Demon: Soul Leech- lifesteal (heal damage done to others)
  • - Cyborg: Unibeam- laser beam
  • - Beast: Rampage- strength 2 + speed 2
  • - Titan: Titan Form- Become a titan (size increase + reach increase)
  • - Human: second chance- Invincibility for 5 seconds
